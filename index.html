About Us

En-Passant: Chess begins from here!

The Enpassant Chess Community at ABES Engineering College is a vibrant group of chess enthusiasts dedicated to promoting the game on campus. Known for organizing exciting events like the ABES Chess Championship and Knightmare, Enpassant fosters a love for chess through friendly competition, skill-building, and community engagement. Their efforts have made chess more popular and accessible among students.
